# Prompt for Perplexity — image research

Copy everything below the line into Perplexity.

---

I'm art-directing the photography for a boutique Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) advisory website. Search the web and find **modern, evocative, license-free images** (Unsplash, Pexels, Pixabay or equivalent free-license sources only) for the image slots listed below.

**Visual identity (non-negotiable):** warm stone / aged-paper palette (cream, sand, taupe, muted teal accent), editorial and calm, classical-architecture undertones (the site features pencil sketches of Greek/Roman capitals). Photos will be displayed slightly desaturated, inside fixed-ratio frames, cropped center.

**What to look for:** images that help a visitor *visualize an abstract concept* — metaphors and atmospheres, not literal office clichés. Think: architectural details, light and shadow on stone, hands at work, paper and craft, quiet desks, structural patterns, classical columns meeting modern glass, archives, keys, seals, harbors and logistics seen abstractly.

**What to avoid:** cheesy corporate stock (handshakes, suited people pointing at charts, lock-and-circuit-board "cyber" imagery, blue-toned tech grids), watermarked or rights-managed photos, anything visually loud or saturated.

For EACH of the 18 slots below, propose **2–3 candidate images** with: direct link to the image page, a one-line rationale (why it evokes the concept), and a suggested alt text. Mind the target crop ratio noted per slot.

HOMEPAGE — "What We Do" cards (small, ratio 5:3, landscape):
1. home-governance — Governance & Risk: risk frameworks, ERM routines, internal audit. Concept: order emerging from complexity, solid structure.
2. home-compliance — Compliance & Regulation: GDPR accountability, Italian Mod. 231, defensible evidence. Concept: proof that holds, documents with weight, seals/archives.
3. home-security — Security & Assurance: supplier audit programmes, loss prevention. Concept: vigilance, protection, watchfulness — heritage of a 1953 security firm.
4. home-esg — ESG & Sustainability: CSRD/CSDDD readiness, double materiality. Concept: substance behind sustainability, nature meeting structure.

HOMEPAGE — heritage section (ratio 4:5, portrait):
5. home-heritage — Three generations of family trust, Napoli, Mediterranean heritage turned into modern method. Concept: timeless Italian architectural detail, weathered stone, continuity.

SERVICES HUB — one per row (ratio 4:3):
6. services-governance — governance & risk structure that fits the organisation
7. services-privacy — privacy as daily operational practice, not paperwork
8. services-esg — the governance system behind the ESG report
9. services-supply-chain — supply-chain due diligence, knowing your suppliers (logistics, ports, networks seen abstractly)
10. services-compliance — internal controls living inside real operations
11. services-assurance — third-party assurance & security oversight, operational depth
12. services-digital — digital GRC: organised data, spreadsheets done right, no shiny platform worship
13. services-fractional — fractional GRC officer: a steady trusted presence at the table

CASE STUDIES (ratio ~4:3):
14. cases-erm — ERM framework expanded across Southern-European logistics entities
15. cases-audit — supplier audit programme redesigned for a 30+ provider network
16. cases-loss-prevention — loss prevention governance across a multi-site distribution network
17. cases-gdpr — GDPR readiness mapped onto real operations

CONTACT (ratio 4:3):
18. contact-desk — a quiet consultancy desk prepared for a thoughtful conversation; warm light, paper, no screens dominating.

Deliver the result as a table grouped by slot. Prioritize visual consistency across the whole set: if the 18 winners were placed side by side they should look like one curated collection — same warmth, same calm, same editorial tone.
