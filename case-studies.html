ERM Framework Expansion

E-commerce logistics, Southern Europe · 2021

Third-Party Audit Programme Redesign

Context: A network of 30+ logistics providers required stronger assurance.

Challenge: Findings closed slowly and audit cycles were too long.

  • Redesigned audit standards and evidence requirements.
  • Built remediation governance with owner accountability.
  • Created clearer escalation and supplier performance routines.

Result: €1.2M savings, closure improvement from 25% to 55%, and 30% shorter audit cycles.

Security & Operations

Multi-site logistics network, 5 countries · 2019-2023

Loss Prevention Programme

Context: A distributed network needed consistent security governance.

Challenge: Incident, shrinkage and supplier controls varied by site.

  • Introduced performance governance for guarding providers.
  • Standardised incident and shrinkage review routines.
  • Aligned controls to operational and financial reporting.

Result: 15% incident reduction, 20% shrinkage reduction and around €2.3M annual losses prevented.

GDPR Readiness
