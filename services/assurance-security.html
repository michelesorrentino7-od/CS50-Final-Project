Security Governance

Loss prevention and security operations — governed, not just reported.

Assurance & Security · Operational security governance

Security operations produce data — incident reports, patrol logs, access records, response times. Most organisations collect this data and do very little with it. Security governance means turning that data into visibility: identifying patterns, setting performance expectations, reviewing incidents for systemic cause and holding providers accountable for outcomes, not just activity.

We design security performance frameworks and loss prevention governance models that connect what your security providers do with what your business cares about. This includes KPI design, incident classification, management reporting templates and the governance cadence that keeps security performance on leadership's agenda.

Case in point: a Third-Party Audit Programme we redesigned for 30+ logistics providers produced measurable improvement in audit closure discipline and surfaced operational patterns that a monthly scorecard had missed for two years.
