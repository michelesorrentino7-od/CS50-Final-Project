How We Work

Diagnose → Design → Embed

A structured approach drawn from a decade inside complex European matrix organisations — adapted to the scale and language of your team.

Diagnose

We start with how the organisation actually operates. Interviews, process walkthroughs and document review across legal, finance and operations — to understand real exposure, not theoretical risk.

Design

We translate obligations, risks and gaps into a lean, auditable framework. Risk register, control map, KRI set, escalation paths and reporting templates — built in the tools your team already uses.

Embed

We transfer ownership to your team with documentation, workshops and where relevant, automation of evidence and monitoring using existing software, data pipelines or AI-assisted review.

Multidisciplinary: legal, finance and operations in one conversation. Grounded in cross-functional practice across European matrix organisations. Hands-on with technology — from GRC tools to AI-driven risk and data analysis.

In Practice

ERM Framework Expansion

Governance & Risk · Southern European logistics operations

A regional logistics operator needed to expand its enterprise risk framework across multiple Southern European entities. Controls were informal, ownership was unclear and the board had no reliable view of operational exposure.

We designed a unified risk taxonomy, mapped controls to named owners across functions, selected a KRI set aligned to the business model and built a management dashboard the leadership team could update monthly without external support.

Outcome: a board-ready risk reporting pack, clear remediation discipline across three entities and an internal team capable of maintaining the framework independently.
