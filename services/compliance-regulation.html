The Gap

Big firms write compliance programmes. We build compliance into how you work.

Large consultancies design frameworks that look impressive in a board presentation. They hand over a manual, run a training session and move on. Twelve months later the manual is out of date, the training is forgotten and the control is not being run.

We work differently. Compliance that holds up is compliance that lives inside your operations — assigned to real people, tied to real processes and checked on a regular cadence. That is what we build.

Italian Specifics

Legislative Decree 231 / 2001

Compliance & Internal Controls · Italian regulatory framework

Mod. 231 creates organisational criminal liability for a defined list of predicate offences — corruption, fraud, environmental violations, workplace safety breaches and others. The defence requires demonstrating that the company had an appropriate compliance programme (Modello Organizzativo e di Gestione) and a functioning Supervisory Body (Organismo di Vigilanza) that actively monitored it.

A Mod. 231 model that exists only on paper is not a defence. We help companies design models that are proportionate to their structure, genuinely implemented in operations and maintained with documented evidence of monitoring. We also support the Organismo di Vigilanza in its oversight function.

We have built and reviewed Mod. 231 compliance models across logistics, security services and multi-entity corporate groups. The standard we apply: it must be defensible in front of an examining magistrate.
