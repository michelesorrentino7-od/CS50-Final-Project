Our Heritage

Three generations.
One mission.

Sorrentino Consultancy connects seventy years of family security heritage, deep entrepreneurial roots in the territory, and over a decade of international corporate GRC experience into one advisory practice.

We work with organisations that need governance that holds up: clear accountability, defensible evidence and practical frameworks built around how the business actually operates — not around how we wish it did.

From banks and institutions to logistics operators and multinationals across five countries, the thread is constant: reliability, discretion, accountability.

For SMEs

Make sense of your risks

Designed for small and mid-sized companies that want structure without bureaucracy.

Lightweight frameworks for fast teams

We help founders put in place just-enough policies, decision forums and reporting so investors, boards and regulators trust the numbers — without slowing product and growth.

Built on matrix-organisation experience, simplified for early-stage teams.

Local rules, global consistency

From entity set-up to ongoing compliance routines and reporting.
